Did You Know?

Pirate's would speak in the language of their homeland. If they were Spaniards, they'd speak Spanish, or another language in a Spanish accent. Contrary to popular culture, pirates didn't say "Arghh, "matey" "ahoy" "scurvy dog," "shiver me timbers" "yo ho ho" or "walk the plank.
